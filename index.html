Ambulance Service

Medical issues can arise unexpectedly, even with people who are in relatively good health. Many such illnesses can be treated in an outpatient medical facility. But there are a number of health conditions that require immediate medical assistance. The only correct decision in such cases is to call an ambulance. The first responders at HealthCare Hospital's ambulance service provide round-the-clock assistance in the event of any medical emergency.

Emergency Rooms

Emergency care is available for adults and children 24/7.You can access our separate adult and pediatric emergency rooms from Prospect Avenue. A patient drop-off and pick-up area is available near the entrance. After drop-off, parking is available in parking structure P4 on eranakulam road (across the street).

Free Checkups

Regular health exams and tests can help find problems before they start. They also can help find problems early, when your chances for treatment and cure are better. Which exams and screenings you need depends on your age, health and family history, and lifestyle choices such as what you eat, how active you are, and whether you smoke.
